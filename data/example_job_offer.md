# Senior Machine Learning Engineer

**Company:** TechVision AI
**Location:** San Francisco, CA (Hybrid)
**Employment Type:** Full-Time
**Salary Range:** $180,000 - $250,000 + Equity
**Posted:** January 15, 2024

## About TechVision AI

TechVision AI is a rapidly growing startup revolutionizing computer vision and deep learning applications in autonomous systems. Backed by top-tier venture capital firms with $50M in Series B funding, we're building the next generation of AI-powered visual intelligence platforms. Our technology powers autonomous vehicles, robotics, and smart city infrastructure across the globe.

## Role Overview

We are seeking an experienced Senior Machine Learning Engineer to join our core ML team. You will work on cutting-edge computer vision problems, deploy models at scale, and help shape the technical direction of our AI platform. This is a high-impact role where you'll collaborate with world-class engineers and researchers to push the boundaries of what's possible with visual AI.

## Key Responsibilities

- Design, develop, and deploy state-of-the-art deep learning models for computer vision applications
- Build scalable ML pipelines capable of processing petabytes of visual data
- Optimize model performance for edge deployment on resource-constrained devices
- Lead technical design reviews and mentor junior engineers
- Collaborate with product teams to translate business requirements into ML solutions
- Contribute to research publications and represent the company at AI conferences
- Establish ML engineering best practices and drive technical excellence

## Required Qualifications

### Education & Experience
- MS/PhD in Computer Science, Machine Learning, or related field (or equivalent experience)
- 5+ years of industry experience in machine learning engineering
- 3+ years specifically in computer vision and deep learning
- Proven track record of deploying ML models to production at scale

### Technical Skills
- **Deep Learning:** Expert knowledge of CNNs, Vision Transformers, and modern architectures
- **Frameworks:** Proficiency in PyTorch or TensorFlow, with production deployment experience
- **Programming:** Strong Python skills and software engineering best practices
- **MLOps:** Experience with ML pipelines, experiment tracking, and model versioning
- **Cloud/Infrastructure:** AWS/GCP experience, Docker, Kubernetes
- **Computer Vision:** Object detection, segmentation, tracking, 3D vision
- **Edge Computing:** Model optimization, quantization, pruning for deployment

### Core Competencies
- Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work with ambiguous requirements
- Excellent communication skills for technical and non-technical audiences
- Experience leading technical projects and mentoring team members
- Passion for staying current with latest ML research and techniques

## Preferred Qualifications

- Publications in top-tier ML/CV conferences (CVPR, ICCV, NeurIPS, ICML)
- Experience with autonomous systems, robotics, or AR/VR applications
- Knowledge of C++ for performance-critical implementations
- Contributions to open-source ML projects
- Experience with multi-modal learning (vision + language/audio)
- Background in 3D computer vision or neural rendering
